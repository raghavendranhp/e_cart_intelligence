You will receive a JSON or text-based summary containing:
- Top-selling products and overall category popularity.

2. Sales & Regional Insights
- Identify the core drivers of revenue.
- Point out any underperforming categories or regions that require attention.

- Propose one specific, actionable marketing campaign for "High-Value Customers" (e.g., loyalty rewards).
- Propose one specific tactic to re-engage "Occasional Buyers".
- Propose one clearance or flash-sale tactic for "Discount Seekers".

FORMATTING CONSTRAINTS:
- Use markdown formatting with clear headers for each of the 4 sections above.
- Use bullet points for readability. Keep sentences short and data-driven.
- DO NOT include any introductory filler (e.g., "Here is your report") or concluding remarks. Start immediately with the "Executive Summary" heading and end after the "Targeted Marketing Strategy" section.
